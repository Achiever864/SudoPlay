# 🧩 Sudoku Challenge

A brain-stretching competitive Sudoku game built for people who love solving puzzles, beating their own records, and proving their geniuses.

No long story. Just open the game, pick your difficulty, solve the puzzle, and climb the leaderboard.

---

## 🎮 About The Game

The goal is simple:

> Fill the grid correctly, solve faster, make fewer mistakes, and become one of the best solvers on the leaderboard.

---

## ✨ Features

### ⚡ Instant Play Experience
You don't need to create an account before playing.

The game automatically creates a guest profile for you, allowing you to:

* Start playing immediately
* Track your scores
* Compete on the leaderboard
* Save your progress

When you are ready, you can create an account and keep your achievements forever.

### 🏆 Competitive Leaderboard

Think you are a Sudoku beast?

Let's see it.

The leaderboard tracks:
* Highest scores
* Fastest completion times
* Best performances by difficulty

Compete with other players and push yourself to the top.

### 🎯 Different Difficulty Levels

Everyone starts somewhere.

Choose your challenge:

* 🟢 Easy
* 🟡 Medium
* 🔴 Hard
* 🟣 Expert

As your confidence grows, increase the difficulty and test your limits.

### 🔥 Personal Progress
Every game helps you improve.

Track:

* Games played
* Completed puzzles
* Highest scores
* Personal records
* Best completion times

Small improvements become big wins.

---

## 🧠 The Idea Behind The Game

The idea was simple:

"Why should solving Sudoku just be about finishing a puzzle?"

A little competition makes everything more interesting.

The aim is to combine:

* Learning
* Problem solving
* Competition
* Personal improvement

into one enjoyable experience.
